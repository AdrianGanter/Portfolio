# SEOC Dashboard – PowerShell Automation Project

## Overview
As part of my role within the Security and Emergency Operations Center (SEOC), I developed a **custom PowerShell-based dashboard** (with assistance from Microsoft Copilot) to streamline day-to-day operations in a **high-pressure, multi-system environment**.

SEOC controllers are required to constantly switch between multiple applications, communication tools, and information sources. This dashboard was designed to **reduce friction, improve response times, and enhance operational efficiency**, particularly in time-critical situations such as emergencies.

This project reflects my interest in **automation, workflow optimisation, and improving operational systems**.

## Problem Statement

SEOC operations require:
- Constant switching between multiple applications (CCTV, access control, communications, logs, etc.)
- Fast access to critical information during emergencies  
- Repetitive manual tasks (e.g., typing dispatch messages, locating contacts, opening systems)

These factors introduced **delays, inefficiencies, and increased cognitive load**, particularly during high-pressure incidents.

---

## Solution

I developed a **centralised dashboard interface** that consolidates tools, resources, and workflows into a **single, user-friendly control panel**, enabling:

- One-click application launches  
- Rapid access to critical operational information  
- Automated text templates for dispatch  
- Real-time note-taking and logging  

---

## Key Features

### 🚀 One-Click System Launch ("Rocket Button")
- Launches all core operational applications simultaneously  
- Eliminates manual startup process  
- Ensures controllers are operational within seconds at shift start  

---

### 📋 Logs & Core Functions
- Quick access buttons for:
  - **Emergency Log**
  - **Running Log**
- Supports faster navigation to critical reporting tools

### 🛠 Tools & Applications
Dedicated quick-launch buttons for frequently used systems, including:
- SAM (accommodation management)
- Command systems
- Safety Culture / iAuditor
- Weatherzone
- Citrix
- Microsoft Teams  

Reduces time spent navigating between systems and improves workflow efficiency.

---

### 📁 Resources Section
Centralised access to critical operational information:

- **Contacts**
  - Editable contact list within the dashboard  
  - Automatically saves changes on edit/exit  

- **Village Information**
  - Key details for all supported sites and villages  

- **Emergency Pickup Points**
  - Quickly accessible and editable reference information  

- **Maps**
  - One-click access to site and village maps  
  - Maps enhanced with **CCTV camera locations and TV reference points**, improving:
    - Incident tracking  
    - Trespasser monitoring  
    - Coordination with ground teams

### 📡 Dispatch Templates (Bottom Bar)
- One-click copy-to-clipboard templates for common jobs (e.g. room access, lockouts)  
- Eliminates repetitive typing  
- Ensures **consistent, accurate communication**  
- Speeds up dispatch during high-pressure situations  

---

### 📝 Integrated Notepad (Core Feature)

The central feature of the dashboard is a **real-time digital notepad**, designed to replace traditional pen-and-paper workflows.

**Key capabilities:**
- Persistent note-taking for active jobs and incidents  
- Auto-save functionality (including on exit)  
- Easy copy/paste for dispatch and reporting  
- Zoom functionality (Ctrl + Scroll) for readability  

This feature supports:
- Faster information capture  
- Improved clarity during incident handling  
- Reduced risk of lost or incomplete information
