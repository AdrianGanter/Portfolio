# Security & Emergency Operations Center (SEOC) – BHP WAIO

## Overview
This workflow outlines my operational experience working within a **Security and Emergency Operations Center (SEOC)** supporting BHP Western Australia Iron Ore (WAIO).  

SEOC functions as a **24/7 centralised command and control environment**, responsible for monitoring, incident response coordination, and real-time intelligence across **9 operational sites and 9 residential villages**.

My role required operating in a **high-pressure, multi-system environment**, managing security threats, emergency incidents, and critical communications while maintaining situational awareness across geographically dispersed operations.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Monitor **CCTV systems (Milestone)** for security incidents, hazard identification, and investigations  
- Manage **duress alarms and access control (Gallagher)**  
- Coordinate **emergency response** (medical, fire, security) via radio and phone systems  
- Perform **live incident tracking** (e.g., trespassers) using CCTV and radio coordination  
- Conduct **CCTV investigations** and process data requests  
- Maintain **accurate incident logs and reporting (iAuditor)**  
- Provide **24/7 operational support** to residents across 9 villages  
- Perform **welfare checks** and support missing person investigations  
- Liaise with **police and emergency services**  
- Monitor and respond to **extreme weather events**  
- Deliver **clear and structured radio communications across multiple regions**  
- Support **training and capability uplift** for incident response and dispatch  

---

## Systems & Technologies

### Security & Monitoring
- **Milestone XProtect** – CCTV monitoring, investigation, and video evidence
- **Gallagher** – Access control and duress alarm monitoring
- **SafeZone** – Lone worker safety monitoring
- **Weatherzone** – Extreme weather tracking

### Communications & Dispatch
- **TETRA Radio Console** – Multi-channel monitoring (Security, ESO, Emergency)
- **Cisco Telephony** – Emergency calls, resident enquiries
- **Omniguard** – Dispatch coordination and real-time tracking

### Incident & Data Management
- **iAuditor** – Incident logging and reporting
- **SAM** – Resident accommodation and personnel information
- **Microsoft 365 (Excel, Outlook)** – Data tracking, intelligence gathering, reporting
- **Webex / Microsoft Teams** – Operational communication and shift handover
