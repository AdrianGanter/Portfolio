I am unable to confirm if person clicked the malicious link and entered any credentials. To be safe, a credential rotation may be necessary, and a chat with the person around visiting suspicious websites and detecting phishing. Escalating to L2 to further investigate.

## Key Takeaways and Learning Points
- More throrough investigating into whether malicious links were actually clicked
- Identifying IP addresses on blacklists and reporting whether the firewall actually blocked it
- Successful blocks from firewall may not require escalation
- Remember to capture all the 5 W's (Who What When Where Why)
- Deeper investigating into spoofed emails to determine it's source
